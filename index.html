MY LIFE SO FAR

I was born in Antwerp, Belgium on 8 januari 1991 and can honestly say "life can give you lemons, up to you to make lemonade".
I have seen the good, the bad and the awesome parts of life.

THE BAD PARTS

So the bad parts...

Trauma is a peculiar thing no? It's something that happens to you, cuts you in a deep place, deprives you of confidence and pride in yourself. It's heavy to carry, can make you start falling behind in life, which gives you more pain to carry... . This is how some people live most of their life, sometimes their entire life. How sad is that :(.

Trauma is what happened in my case;I spent most of my youth struggling, fighting and running around scared and lonely, only to become more and more lost and clueless, each passing year.
From age 13 to 26, I felt trapped, alone, became depressed, was haunted by my own demons and felt oh so very abandoned...

THE GOOD PARTS

The good parts however...

Because of my stubbornness and drive for betterment, I explored in depth a large range of interests and hobbies. No friends or girls frees up a lot of time 😂:

  • Played music since I was four, mastered artists like Rachmaninoff and Chopin in my academical prime.
  • Did intense sports: competetive swimming when I was a teenager, body building when I was 23.
  • Followed university educations, studying both chemistry and mathematics.
  • Went backpacking and grape picking fervently, reconnecting with nature and myself periodically.
  • Grew philosophical, thinking and debating on moral and ethical issues.

THE AWESOME PARTS

The best part...

Was my very own period of enlightenment, which taught me a whole range of mystical truths of life.

At age 26, I finally saw that the way forward was inwards: I stopped my mathematical studies, became what I would call a modern-day monk, lived in a tiny studio working some menial jobs, while focusing on self healing and meditation, and in two years time became transformed, born anew, ready to begin challenge life's next chapter.

In this new chapter of mine, things have been pretty awesome until now, although challenging at times as well: I met a girl, we started a family, bought a house, began my career in IT, am growing and learning each day, and am ready to take on the world!!!

"Life still gives me lemons, but hopefully, I'll only be making more lemonade from now on."
